Information We Collect

When you interact with our services, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and physical address. If you represent a business, we may also collect company details like the business name and industry. Additionally, login credentials, purchase history, and service preferences may be recorded to enhance your experience. Automatically collected data may include your IP address, device type, browser type, and operating system, as well as pages viewed, time spent on the site, and referral sources. With your consent, general geographic information may also be collected.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to fulfill orders, provide customer support, and keep you informed about updates and purchases. With your consent, we may also send personalized marketing messages. The data we collect helps us optimize our website, improve user experience, and detect and prevent fraudulent activities. Ensuring the security of your interactions is a priority in all our processes.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to enhance your browsing experience. These files allow functionalities such as remembering login details, understanding user behavior, and personalizing content. We use essential cookies to maintain the functionality of our website, performance cookies to analyze usage, and advertising cookies to deliver relevant promotions. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, though doing so may impact the usability of our website.

Sharing Your Information

Your personal information is never sold, rented, or traded. However, to provide our services effectively, we may share your data with payment processors, marketing agencies, and web hosting providers. We may also disclose information to legal authorities when required to comply with legal obligations or enforce our terms.

Marketing Communications

With your explicit consent, we may contact you with updates about our products and services. You can opt out of these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe link provided in our emails or by contacting us at info@skilwize.com. Please note that transactional emails, such as purchase confirmations, will still be sent as they are necessary for service delivery.

Protecting Your Data

We adopt rigorous security measures to ensure the protection of your personal information. All data transmissions are encrypted via SSL- secured channels, and access to sensitive information is tightly controlled. Regular audits and security updates are conducted to maintain a secure environment.

Your Rights

You have the right to access and request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. If any information is inaccurate, you may request corrections or updates. Should you wish to delete your personal data or exercise your right to data portability, we will assist in providing the information in a commonly used format. You also have the right to object to certain data processing activities. For any such requests, please contact us at info@skilwize.com.

This privacy policy is subject to periodic updates. Changes will be reflected on this page, and significant updates will be communicated through our website or via email when applicable.
